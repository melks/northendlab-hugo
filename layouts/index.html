About Richard

Musician

Richard earned his BA in Music at Yale University.

Since then, he has been involved in music his whole life, performing regularly in groups of all sizes, and leading the Jon Hamilton Big Band as Musical Director for the past eight years.

He plays regularly in the five-piece swing band The Night Owls, which can be seen performing all-year round in the South East of the U.K.

Teacher

Richard has taught saxophone, clarinet, and music theory for over a decade, and has successfully taken students through dozens of graded exams (ABRSM and Trinity).

He holds a current DBS check, and is available for virtual or in-person lessons.
